Hello there? If you are not here by accident, you probably know me. In any case, I am Vitalis, Kipyegon Kirui Vitalis, a full-stack software developer or a software engineer if you might call it. There a lot about me but I satisfied introducing myself with these words. My favorite and choice of stack is MEAN stack, yes, MEAN.

If you are a software developer, you probably know that they stack is not the first choice for many people. If not saying it's not popular but the trend is not rising compared to other stacks. At least the Angular part, I believe a number of people out there prefers Vue Js || React Js over angular. Personally, I love Angular and if you ask me, I will always choose Angular any day any minute.

For new developers and those who wish to venture in software development field, I am not endorsing Angular, I just saying my personal favorite. Don't make a decision using it, believe me, I haven't tried either of the framework and the library.

Anyway, this is my personal blog, developed the entire system using MEAN stack. This is where I think loud, share ideas, engage with you guys, argue software development topics, learn from you good people, meet clients among other things we are going to explore. Cheers.
